Let me tell a bit about myself

Right now you most likely wandering, how come that german philologist is working as Web Developer/UI Designer. Well... It is a long story and I do not want you to start yawning, so let me tell you the short one.

I have chosen this studying program because I did not know exactly what I wanted to do and what I wanted to be. After I have successfully obtained a bachelor degree and since I can speak 4 different languages Russian, Latvian, English and German, I decided to become a translator, but it did nit work... However it was not enough for me to feel myself on the right place, so I started a new chapter. In July 2022 I decided to learn how to code and to become a programmer. So I spent months, days, hours just learning new information. I started with some online courses from The Odin Project where I have learned the basics of programming and obviously programming languages, such as: HTML, SCC, JavaScript. The last one is kind of complicated, it means I am still learning it, but I already know a lot (I think :D). THE POWER OF THE INTERNET!

So yes, you have understood everything correct. I am a self-taught Web Developer and UI Designer. And I am extremely proud of myself!
